What We Stand For

We call ourselves the "punks of Western yoga"—not as rebellion for its own sake, but as a commitment to authenticity over marketing, substance over style, and practitioners over profits.

"Teach First, Tool Second"

Every piece of content, every tool, every resource starts with education. We don't sell solutions to manufactured problems. We identify real pain points, explain them thoroughly, and then offer tools that genuinely help.

The directory is free. The education is free. When we build tools, they follow the same philosophy: explain the problem, show the manual solution, then offer something that saves time.

  • Challenging yoga's commercialization without dismissing modern innovation
  • Honoring tradition while remaining accessible to beginners
  • Science-driven credibility without rejecting spirituality
  • Accessible over exclusive—no gatekeeping, no guru worship
  • Sincere over performative—real help over marketing fluff

What We Create

For Practitioners: A comprehensive glossary of 70+ yoga terms, in-depth style guides for every major practice, and 30 essential questions answered by someone who's actually been on the mat for decades.

For Studio Owners: The largest yoga studio directory in North America (30,000+ listings), plus practical guides on SEO, marketing, and business growth—written by someone who understands both the business and the practice.

Everything we make serves one goal: helping people find, understand, and deepen their yoga practice. Whether that means finding a studio around the corner or understanding the difference between Vinyasa and Hatha.
