# Yoga Near Me Glossary: Master Content Enhancements

## STYLE & SEQUENCES
### Yoga for Stress Relief
**What to Expect Snippet:**
- **Parasympathetic Activation:** The primary goal is to shift your body out of "fight or flight" and into the "rest and digest" state (the parasympathetic nervous system).
- **Lower Cortisol:** Expect slow, rhythmic movements and long-held poses that are scientifically proven to lower cortisol levels and reduce heart rate.
- **Emphasis on the Exhale:** Breathwork in these classes will focus on lengthening the exhalation, which acts as a natural "brake" for the nervous system.
- **Mindfulness & Presence:** Instead of a "workout," the focus is on a "work-in"â€”noticing physical sensations and keeping the mind anchored in the present moment.
- **Accessible & Non-Competitive:** These classes prioritize comfort over complexity; the goal is to feel better, not to "master" a difficult posture.

### Yoga for Flexibility
**What to Expect Snippet:**
- **Dynamic vs. Static Stretching:** A mix of "flowing" stretches and "held" poses that target connective tissues.
- **Consistency over Intensity:** Gained through regular, gentle practice rather than pushing into pain.

### Prenatal Yoga
**What to Expect Snippet:**
- **Modified Postures:** Avoid lying flat on your back or deep twists; plenty of room for the baby.
- **Breath for Labor:** Focus on techniques designed to help you stay calm during delivery.

## TECHNIQUES & BREATHWORK
### Nadi Shodhana (Alternate Nostril Breathing)
**How to Practice Snippet:**
- **Balancing the Hemispheres:** Designed to balance the logical and emotional sides of the brain.
- **Nervous System Reset:** Helps stimulate the parasympathetic nervous system for immediate calm.

### Kapalbhati (Skull Shining Breath)
**How to Practice Snippet:**
- **The "Pump":** Forceful, short exhalations driven by snapping the low belly inward.
- **Mental Clarity:** Believed to clear "brain fog" and increase oxygen to the frontal lobe.

## ENERGY & PHILOSOPHY
### Prana (Life Force Energy)
**How to Practice Snippet:**
- **The "Vital Spark":** The electricity that runs through the body's "wiring."
- **Breath as the Vehicle:** Breath is how you catch and direct this energy.
