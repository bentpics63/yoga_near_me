\#\# 2\. THE BRAND IDENTITY: "The Concierge"  
To transform \*\*yoganearme.info\*\*, we shift the "personality" of the site:  
\*   \*\*Old Identity:\*\* A directory that gives you a list (Passive).  
\*   \*\*New Identity:\*\* A curated guide that gives you a recommendation (Active).  
\*   \*\*The Voice:\*\* Professional, poetic, scientifically grounded, and inclusive. (e.g., \*“Saying you're too stiff for yoga is like saying you're too dirty to take a bath.”\*)

\#\#\# \*\*Labeling & Navigation\*\*  
\*   \*\*Avoid:\*\* "FAQ," "Listings," "Search."  
\*   \*\*Adopt:\*\* "The Manual," "The Collection," "Sanctuaries," "The Flow."  
\*   \*\*Navigation Placement:\*\* Do not put "The Manual" in the top-level primary nav. Place it in the \*\*Footer\*\* or a \*\*"Guides"\*\* dropdown to keep the focus on studio discovery.

\#\# 3\. TECHNICAL SEO & URL STRUCTURE  
To rank for long-tail keywords and be discoverable by AI/LLMs (ChatGPT/Gemini), use this structure:

\*   \*\*URL Pattern:\*\* \`yoganearme.info/answers/\[question-slug\]/\`  
\*   \*\*Page Layout:\*\*  
    1\.  \*\*H1:\*\* The exact question.  
    2\.  \*\*Snippet Bait:\*\* A 40–60 word direct answer (in a blockquote or box).  
    3\.  \*\*The Deep Dive:\*\* 800–1,200 words of "Concierge" style content.  
    4\.  \*\*Visuals:\*\* 2+ high-quality lifestyle images \+ 1 embedded YouTube video.  
    5\.  \*\*Schema:\*\* Must use \*\*FAQ JSON-LD Schema\*\* markup.
