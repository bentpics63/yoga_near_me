About Our Studio

We love helping people feel their best. Our studio offers a welcoming environment for practitioners of all levels, from complete beginners to advanced yogis. Our experienced teachers bring decades of combined practice to every class, focusing on proper alignment, breath awareness, and the transformative power of consistent practice.

Whether you're looking to build strength, increase flexibility, reduce stress, or simply find a moment of peace in your busy day, Yoga Academy has a class for you.

First Visit Information

What to Bring
Yoga mat (rentals $3) Towel for hot classes Water bottle Comfortable clothing

First-time visitors: Please arrive 15 minutes early to complete registration and get oriented to the studio. Our studio is located on the 2nd floor with elevator access available. Changing rooms and lockers are provided at no extra charge.
